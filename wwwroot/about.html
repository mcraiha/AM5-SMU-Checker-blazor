About

AM5 SMU Checker with Blazor is free, open source, does not contain ads and does not collect any info from users.

Free

Usage of AM5 SMU Checker with Blazor is free and always will be free.

Open source

All components of AM5 SMU Checker with Blazor are released under permissive open source licenses.

Ad free

AM5 SMU Checker with Blazor does not show any ads for users.

Privacy

AM5 SMU Checker with Blazor does not collect any info from users. All inputs are processed inside your browser.

Tech

AM5 SMU Checker with Blazor is powered by HTML5, Javascript and WASM.
